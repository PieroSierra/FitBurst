Privacy Policy for FitBurst

The FitBurst app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Data Collection

  • No Personal Data Collected: FitBurst does not collect any personal information, such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • No Tracking or Analytics: FitBurst does not gather or track any data, including anonymized usage statistics or analytics.
  • No Access to Device Features: FitBurst does not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or any other sensitive data.

Third-Party Services

  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: Since FitBurst does not collect any personal data or display any advertising, it do not share any data with third-party services.

User Control

  • No Permissions Required: FitBurst does not require any permissions from users, as it does not interact with sensitive data or device features.
